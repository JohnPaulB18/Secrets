Our Policy



Data we collect automatically when you use of our Services
When you interact with our Platform or use our Services, we automatically collect the following information about you:


Location information
Depending on your device permissions, if you opt to locate the nearest inspection centre, we automatically collect and process information about your actual location. We use various technologies to determine location, including IP address, GPS, Wi-Fi access points and mobile towers. Your location data allows you to see inspection centres near you and helps in suggesting inspection centres near you.


Cookies and Similar Technologies
We use cookies to manage our users’ sessions, to store your preferences language selection and deliver you relevant advertisements. "Cookies" are small text files transferred by a web server to the hard drive of your device. Cookies may be used to collect the date and time of your visit, your browsing history, your preferences, and your username. You can set your browser to refuse all or some cookies, or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. If you disable or refuse cookies, please note that some parts of our Services/Platform may become inaccessible or not function properly.


Vehicle Related Information
We check, what is the status of your vehicle from, (i) Ministry of Road Transport & Highways, Government of India’ website which at present is called ‘Vahan NR e-services’, and (ii) from State/Central Government websites which display vehicular challan, fines and legal matters status.


Credit Information
We check/obtain your credit information from third party sources.


Call Recording
If our representative calls you on the telephone or if you call us on the telephone to discuss your car, we will record such call for training and internal quality purposes.
